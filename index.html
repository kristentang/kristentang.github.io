About My Summer

This summer I will be a TA for the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at Warner Brothers in Burbank. It is a 7 week program for incoming 11th and 12th grade girls, that encourages them to pursue computer science by teaching them coding skills through a project based curriculum and demonstrating real-world applications of computer science. I will be part of a teaching team whose goal is to teach the curriculum and manage our classroom, encourage and mentor the students, and build a supportive and collaborative learning environment. I have experience programming in C++, Matlab, Java, and Python. While I haven't had any formal classroom experience in web development, I made this website as preparation for TAing the Summer Immersion Program. Stay tuned, I will be using this website to blog my experience weekly!
